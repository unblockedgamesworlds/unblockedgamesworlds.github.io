How to play:Zombie Shooter game consists of 15 chapters in total. After pressing the play button to start the zombie shooter game, start the game by selecting the first chapter from the chapter selection that appears. The aim here is to shoot the zombies in different parts of the platform with the bullets bouncing from the gun in your hand. The more zombies you shoot with fewer bullets, the more points you get. Don't get excited and finish all the bullets. The more zombies, the more points. Have fun.
